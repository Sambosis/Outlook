Good afternoon,

Here are some quick notes for when filling out the PL tab on your 2025 budget.

  1. Many line items will have nothing entered.  Look at last years budget against your Q3 2024 Financials and we can discuss if needed.
  2. Sales Training will most likely be the first tab you will need to enter a dollar amount.  For all branches you can plan to spend $140 per quarter for Sandler.  You should also budget $250 for any new OSR hire to attend the Sandler Boot Camp.  Look to your GL for any other money you feel should be allocated here. 
  3. No need to budget anything for Restaurant Shows, Customer Relations, donations or Advertising unless there is something you want to discuss at the meetings
  4. For Branch Office training you would need to budget $ if you are hiring a new Office Admin or if you would like to request Katrina to come out and train an existing person in that position.
  5. Dues and Subscriptions.  Look at your GL to see what is being spent here.  This column is very small if anything at many branches.
  6. Travel and Entertainment we will discuss.  For  now put it what you feel is the correct number and as always be able to explain where the money allocated is being spent.
  7. Presidents Club.  Enter $7,500 per OSR that will be going next year from your branch.  If no one is going keep it a $0.

 

For your input tab here are just a few notes:

For income per install use your projected new sales and what you feel the average install fee will be.  Most branched did well this year on getting solid install fees.

For Avg Energy Surcharge go off your financials while incorporating projected net gain.  Same should be done for branches that use the Major Account Surcharge.

For Franchise fee all branches use 4.96% for now.

For both Dishracks-Profit % and SS & Mice Profit % put in 40% for now

For all other line items we will need to look into this year’s financials and build our number off of that.  On some we may need to raise our spending and others curb it.  Most important is that you can explain the number and why.  Here is a quick example of what that means:

 

For the line item Shop Expense – Uniform. Shoes PPE look at your GL and see what your Uniform Allowance is per period so you can incorporate that.  You will then look at cost per pants/shirts/jackets/etc that you project to buy in 2025 (link to clean uniform store included).  Next estimate what you will spend on shoes and PPE and you will have a solid base # for your spending on this line.
