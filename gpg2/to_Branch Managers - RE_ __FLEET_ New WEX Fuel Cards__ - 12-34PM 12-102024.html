Good morning,

Roll-out and implementation of our new WEX Fuel Card w/ Roadside Service will begin this week.  By Thursday all branches will be receiving a FedEx envelope containing the following:

  • WEX Fuel Card utilization information (for posting)
  • WEX Roadside Service utilization information (for posting)
  • Adhesive visor card-holders (to go in each service vehicle)
  • Driver roster and NEW ID#s to utilize the fuel cards

 

As soon as you receive this envelope, the fuel cards may be distributed to their specific vehicle along with the card-holders.  Please adhere the card-holder to the driver’s-side visor of all service vehicles.  This will help ensure that cards are kept in their vehicle’s. 

 

Driver ID#s can then also be distributed to each driver.  The best way would be to cut the roster into strips, one strip for each driver and then hand them to them.  This will give them a physical copy for reference until the new number is memorized.  Fleet Services (Maggie & myself) have access to the MASTER list of ID#s.  Please do NOT post the list of Driver ID#s or read them out aloud to distribute. 

 

Contact me or Maggie if you have any questions.
