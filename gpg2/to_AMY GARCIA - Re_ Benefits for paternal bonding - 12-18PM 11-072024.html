There are two parts to a leave of absence:  1) Job Protection and 2) Wage Replacement Programs

 

Job Protection Leave

 

Federal and State leave options and eligibility (click on blue links for more information):

 

You are currently not eligible for FMLA (Family Medical Leave Act - federal job protection leave) or NJ-FLA (NJ Family Leave Act - state job protection leave) until next April 2025 because you haven’t worked for us for one year.  FMLA and NJFLA run concurrently for the father. In March 2025, we can re-review your options for when you become eligible for those programs.

 

Auto-Chlor System understands the importance of being with your new baby and for employees who are not eligible for job protection leaves, we offer an unpaid, nonjob-protected two-week personal leave for you to bond with your baby.   If the employee does not return from a leave of absence, it’s considered a voluntary resignation.  A personal leave can only be requested once in a rolling year period.

 

Please fill out the attached LOA request form and check that you want to take a personal leave. Please send this back to me ASAP and I will schedule a meeting for us to review your leave.

 

Wage Replacement Programs

 

While you are on your two-week personal leave you may be eligible for wage replacement through NJ State called Family Leave Insurance: NJ-FLI

You can also decide to use any accrued time (vacation/floating holiday) to replace your wages while you are out.
