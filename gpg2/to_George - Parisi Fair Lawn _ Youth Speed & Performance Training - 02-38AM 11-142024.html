More than 4 million young athletes suffer from sports-related injuries every single year,* and more than 80% of these injuries involve the body’s connective tissue and fascia system. Young athletes just aren’t prepared for the intensity and unpredictable movement required in training… but it doesn’t have to be this way. The Parisi Speed School has been dedicated for over 30 years to help athletes become more resilient and perform at their highest levels.

We’ve teamed up with our partners at Alliance Orthopedic to develop the Performance and Protection Evaluation to design the best training program for YOUR athlete. We examine an athlete’s current speed, power and movement literacy, along with a musculoskeletal system to identify imbalances, movement limitations and asymmetries that can hamper performance and lead to injury.

This evaluation is designed to create unbreakable athletes who can stay healthy train harder, so they can perform better!
