As we continue to improve our processes, we are updating and refining the labor transfer procedure on the EOP project.

This update introduces changes to streamline the process and ensure better alignment with the positions involved.

  1. New Transfer Categories:
  • Previously, all labor transfers were processed using GL 62305 regardless of the position.

  1. Clearer Alignment:
  • This separation ensures that labor costs are accurately aligned with the respective roles, providing better tracking and reporting.
