Hello All,

 

We are excited to announce that the training videos for the UKG Pro system are now ready for review! As we transition away from ADP to the UKG system, you’ll see that you’ll be able to perform a lot more tasks within UKG, without the use of physical paper. Things like time punch corrections, time off requests, and timecard reviews are now done electronically through a computer or right on the UKG mobile app.

 

These training videos have been designed to help you get up to speed and make the most of our new UKG system. We would like for you to take a moment to go through them. Note that you will need your Auto-Chlor email address and password to access the videos. Please reach out to the IS Department if you need assistance.

 

You can access the training videos via the following links:

 

  1. UKG Overview
  2. UKG Time Punch
  3. UKG Timecard
  4. WPS Exception Blocking
  5. UKG Time Off Requests
  6. UKG Employee Timecards – Managers Only

 

Please take the time to review these videos at your earliest convenience. There are two go live schedules as below. The system will not be available to you if it hasn’t gone live for your branch.

 

For Biweekly Pay Branches and Plants, the system goes live on December 15th , 2024.

For Weekly Pay Branches, the system goes live on December 22nd , 2024.
