[HOME>OPERATIONS>ROUTE USER]

The “HOME>OPERATIONS>ROUTE USER” module now also moves the handheld to the assigned route.

The module has been updated to better reflect that operation.

 

Step 1:

Select the SSR being moved in the “USER” selecthelper and click the “LOAD” button.

 

Step 2:

Select the route the SSR is being moved to in the “NEW ROUTE” selecthelper and click the “ASSIGN ROUTE” button.

 

Similar to Visiting Driver, the SSR will get a dialogue box to confirm the move.

 

Note:

  1. Route assignment is now only available when there are no transactions on the current or new route during the week.
  2. To make sure route swap smoothly, please make sure both SSRs are at the branch.
