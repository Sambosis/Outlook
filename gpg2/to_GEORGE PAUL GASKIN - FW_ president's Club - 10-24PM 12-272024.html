One thing to keep in mind is that if accounts are being billed today that were billed in periods 5-13 it will change these numbers!  So as an example if Bassel, Tom or Scott were to have several machines bill today or tomorrow with no companion it may bring their percentage down below the 20% qualifier.  We have been talking about this for the last several weeks so keep an eye on the numbers and be sure to bill all customers correctly. 

 

To be clear on how the number is calculated do the following:

  1. Run the presidents club report
  2. Take the big 5 number and subtract the companion number.  So if big 5 was $20,000 and companion was $4,000 it would be $16,000
  3. Divide the companion number (it was $4,000) by the calculated big 5 number ($16,000) to come up with the percentage.  In this example it would be $4,000 divided by $16,000 = 25%.
