My favorite cold call opener whether you are front of house or coming through the back.  Always start with “looking to speak to the owner or manager, would that be you?”

When they are in front of you “hey, is now a good time to talk?”(you will be amazed the amount of times people go “yeah, I have a few minutes”  It shifts from you asking for a few minutes to them giving you a few minutes without you asking.  Its magic!)

Then “just stopping in to see if it would be worth a conversation about your dish machine…I’m not even sure who you have right now”(confused, laid back tone.  People love to help)

Whoever they have, lead with common problems.

“Got it.  What we have been hearing a lot lately from other Ecolab customers is 3 main things.
1. They have to re-wash their dishes multiple times because they don’t come clean the first time through or they hand wash in the 3 comp and then use the machine as just a sanitizer
2. When the machine breaks down its taking until the next day or later for them to come out and fix
3. This is the most common one we have been hearing and they have said the pricing for the chemical has gone sky high the last few years. 

“curious, what has your experience been with them lately?”

 

Be different, be a pattern interrupt. 

 

Do not give them a chance to put that sales wall up because you started out sounding like the food person or the POS system person.

 

Go in there thinking like a buyer, seeing this process through their POV and it will lead to more conversations, longer conversations, more meetings booked and more sales made
