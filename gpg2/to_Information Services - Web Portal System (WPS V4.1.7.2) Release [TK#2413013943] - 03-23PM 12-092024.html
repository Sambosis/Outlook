A new portal release WPS V4.1.7.2 has been deployed.  The following fixes and features are included in this release:

 

Highlights

  • The "HOME>OPERATIONS>DOT>VEHICLE>SEARCH DOT VEHICLE" now defaults "IS VEHICLE ACTIVE" to "YES".
  • The "HOME>REPORTS>ROUTE SALES REPORTS>CUSTOMER MACHINE WASHES AND PRODUCT SALES" report now shows customers with no products installed.
 

11 additional bug fixes and improvements have been added.  Special thanks to Nick Eddy, Chris Bengtson, Jonah Beckstead, Fred Sills and everyone else involved. 

 

 

[HOME>OPERATIONS>DOT>VEHICLE>SEARCH DOT VEHICLE]

The “IS VEHICLE ACTIVE” dropdown now defaults to “YES”

 

[HOME>REPORTS>ROUTE SALES REPORTS>CUSTOMER MACHINE WASHES AND PRODUCT SALES]

The “PRODUCT SALES LESS THAN” and “PRODUCT COUNT LESS THAN” filters now will also show customers with 0 “PRODUCT SALES” or “NUM PRODUCTS”
