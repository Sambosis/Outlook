The Safety Data Sheet QR-Code Placards are now available to be ordered through IQMS under part # PLCD00021. 

 

Scanning the code will provide a link that will take you to the SDS page of our ACS website.  There you can search for products, scroll through them alphabetically and access the important information when needed. 

 

“According to OSHA, if you choose to provide digital access to Safety Data Sheets (SDS), they must be "readily accessible" to employees without any barriers, meaning no passwords or restrictions that prevent immediate access, and employees must be trained on how to access and use the electronic system; a backup system should also be in place in case of power outages or other emergencies.”

 

A MASTER SDS should still be kept for emergencies.
