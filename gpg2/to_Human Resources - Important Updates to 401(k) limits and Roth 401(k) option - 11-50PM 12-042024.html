Hi ACS Team!

 

Below is important changes and updates to our 401(k) Plan for 2025!

 

Roth 401(k)

We will be offering a ROTH 401(k) option starting on January 1, 2025. 

If you would like to start contributing, you can do by logging into EPIC www.go-retire.com on January 1st and select deferrals to go to Roth 401(k). Roth 401(k) is a post-tax deferral amount.  Click here to learn more: ROTH

You can give to both 401(k) and ROTH 401(k) if you wish but the contributions limit amounts are a combined amount.

 

Contribution Limits for 2025

 

These contributions are counted as a combined total amount if you give both to 401(k) and Roth 401(k).

 

  • 401(k) deferral limit is increasing to $23,500
  • Catch-up contribution
    • You must be 50 years old or older by December 31st to participate.
    • Catch-up contribution limit remains unchanged at $7,500
  • There is a new Super Catch-up starting on January 1, 2025!
    • You must be between the ages of 60 – 63 on December 31, 2025. 
      • If you turn 64 at any time during 2025 you will not be able to take advantage of the new Super Catch-up.
      • If you will be turning 60 on or before December 31, 2025, you can take advantage of the new Super Catch-up in 2025.
    • Super Catch-up limit is $11,250

 

Find your age range below to see your deferral limits for 2025:

  • Ages 18 to 49: limit is $23,500
  • Ages 50-59 and 64 & Older: limit is $23,500 plus $7,500 (catch up) for a total of $31,000
  • Ages 60-63: limit is $23,500 plus $11,250 (super catch up) for a total of $34,750
